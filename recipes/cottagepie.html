Cottage Pie

Description

A simple meal consiting of ground beef

Ingredients

  • mashed potatoes flakes
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 package frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 package frozen corn
  • garlic powder
  • butter/li>
  • milk
  • shredded chedder cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Saut√© ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat and add garlic powder to taste, until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, make the mashed potatoes according to the package instructions.
  4. Dump the contets of the frying pan, including the grease, into an casserole pan. Spread out so that you have an even layer of ground beef.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  6. Layer the frozen carrots, peas and corn.
  7. Layer the mashed potatoes over the frozen vegtables.
  8. Slice half a stick of butter into thin squares and distribute evenly over the top of the mashed potatoes.
  9. Cover the top with a layer of shredded cheese.
  10. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
  11. Let sit for 5-10 minutes, then serve and enjoy!
